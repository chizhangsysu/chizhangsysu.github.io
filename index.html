About Me

I am a PhD candidate from Sun Yat-Sen University, majoring in Computer Aplication Technology, starting from Sep. 2012 and supervised by Prof. Hongyang Chao. I have been interning in Alibaba Inc. in 2011 and 2013. Starting from 2014, my interest has shifted from pure software engineering to image processing related topics. Currently I'm interning at Microsoft Corporation and my main focus is on the stereo matching problem.
