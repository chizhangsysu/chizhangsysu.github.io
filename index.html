Chi Zhang (张弛)

I am a joint PhD candidate between Sun Yat-Sen University (SYSU) and Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) since September, 2012. I am supervised by Prof. Hongyang Chao from SYSU and Dr. Yong Rui from MSRA. I also work closely with Zhiwei Li, who is currently my mentor and a Lead Researcher from the Multimedia Search and Mining Group of MSRA. My current research interests are on Stereo Reconstruction and Depth Map Enhancement.
